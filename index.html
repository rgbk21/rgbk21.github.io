Hi there! I am Gaurav. The motive behind creating this page is more as a service to myself than to you, the reader. At the same time, it serves as a central repository for all things, academic and otherwise, that have interested me over the years and continue to interest me today as well. So, depending on the section you find yourself in, you might be looking at concrete mathematical proofs or the incoherent ramblings of a juvenile delinquent (that's how my major adviser described me by the way. The delinquent part. Not the mathematical proof part. Obviously).

I am currently pursuing my Masters in Computer Science from Iowa State University, US. I completed my undergrad in Electronics and Communications Engineering from National Institute of Technology, Hamirpur, India. I worked for around 6 years in Ericsson after which I decided to pursue higher studies and found myself half way around the world in Ames, from where I am writing this right now.
