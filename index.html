Hi there! I am Gaurav. This page is intended to serve as an index for all the things (projects, notes, code, blogs) that I have created or will create, both in the past and the future. I hope for it to also serve as a central repository for all things, academic and otherwise, that have interested me over the years and continue to interest me today as well. So, depending on the section you find yourself in, you might be looking at concrete mathematical proofs or the semi-incoherent ramblings of a juvenile delinquent.

I am currently pursuing my Masters in Computer Science from Iowa State University, US. I completed my undergrad in Electronics and Communications Engineering from National Institute of Technology, Hamirpur, India. I worked for around 6 years in Ericsson after which I decided to pursue higher studies and found myself half way around the world in Ames, from where I am writing this right now.

One of the primary reasons of creating this page is to maintain an online repository of notes of the lectures that I have really enjoyed during my Masters here. You can find all of these notes in the Notes section on the nav bar. If you have any suggestions for the site, or would just like to chat, feel free to send me a mail: gaurav@iastate.edu
