Lecture 1: Alphabets, basic questions
Basic words in Polish
- Co: What. Co słychać? (What's up?)
- Kto: Who. Kto to jest? (Who is this?). These two (Co and Kto can actually become cofusing in dialogue. Co to jest? (meaning: What is this?). And Kto to jest? (meaning: Who is this?))
- Gdzie: Where. Gdzie mieszkasz? (Where do you live?)
- Kiedy: When. Kiedy weekend? (When is the weekend?)
- Dlaczego: Why.
- Chcę: I want..
- Jak: This can be "How?", "What?", "Which?". It's not a clear conversion. But the answer to this question is always an adjective. Explanation in Lecture 11 video. See notes for lecture 11 below.
- Tutaj / Tu: Here.
- Tam: There
- Teraz: Now/Here/Right now. On diki.
- Twój: Your
- Mój: My
- Ten/Ta/To: Different forms of "This...". The forms are for usage with On/Ona/Ono nouns resp.
- Ja: I
- o: At. O Diki
- do: In, into, and everything in between...
- na: On, at, in, around. The list goes on.
- Czy: Whether. Questions starting with Czy typically have a Tak / Nie (Yes/No) answer.

- z: With
- Tylko: Only
- Albo: Either/Or
- Ale: But
- Ile: How many? / How much?
- Jestem: I am
- Imię / Nazywa: First name / Full name
- Nazwisko: Last name
